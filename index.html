A new way to see

'ThinkFrame' is a community sourced news aggregation/ metadata layer. Due to its decentralised blockchain nature, reader can track the origin, history, context and its legitimacy and with all the classification scores provided by community. The reader is encouraged to form their own opinion on the topic by giving them the opportunity to share their ratings.
